About Us

Chengara Supari is a leading end-to-end initiative in the Indian supari market. We, under Chengara Traders, are a proprietorship trading brand headquartered in Malappuram, Kerala. Chengara Traders was incorporated in 2022 by Shinoop T with decades of cumulative experience in supari trading. We provide the best quality products across 20 states and 4 union territories through online platforms like Flipkart, Meesho, and Amazon. Now we are widening our supply chain through franchisees.

Our Vision

To foster an entrepreneurial ecosystem that nurtures and supports the growth of visionary leaders in India.

Our Mission

To create the most reliable and efficient supply chain for the best quality products from Kerala.

Our Passion

We are committed to creating a supply chain for supari trading across India, standing out for our quality and dedication to customer satisfaction.
